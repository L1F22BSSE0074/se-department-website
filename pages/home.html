Welcome to the Computer Science Department

Department Overview

The Computer Science Department offers high-quality education and research opportunities in computing, software development, artificial intelligence, data science, and modern technologies. Our programs prepare students for successful careers in industry and academia.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science, foster innovation and critical thinking, and contribute to society through research, education, and community engagement.
