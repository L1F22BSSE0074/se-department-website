About the Program

The Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering (BSSE) program is designed to equip students with strong foundations in software development, system design, problem-solving, and emerging technologies. The program focuses on both theoretical knowledge and practical skills required by the software industry.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Intermediate (FSc Pre-Engineering / ICS) or equivalent
  • Minimum percentage as per university admission policy
  • Pass university entry test / interview (if applicable)

Admission Requirements

  • Completed admission application form
  • Academic transcripts (Matric & Intermediate)
  • CNIC / B-Form copy
  • Recent passport-size photographs
Important Note:
Admissions are offered on a merit basis and are subject to availability of seats.

How to Apply

Applicants can apply online through the university admission portal. After submission, shortlisted candidates will be contacted for further evaluation or interview.
